Volume Ctrl: 
Up: KC_KB_VOLUME_UP
Down: KC_KB_VOLUME_DOWN
Mute: KC_KB_MUTE

Brightness Ctrl:
Up: KC_BRIGHTNESS_UP
Down: KC_BRIGHTNESS_DOWN
Sleep Toggle: KC_SYSTEM_SLEEP

Zoom Ctrl:
In (ctrl + "="): LCTL(KC_EQUAL)
Out (ctrl + "-"): LCTL(KC_MINUS)
Reset Zoom toggle (ctrl + 0): LCTL(KC_0)

Horizontal Nav Ctrl ("MOUSEKEY_ENABLE = yes" in rules.mk):
Scroll Left: KC_MS_WH_L
Scroll Right: KC_MS_WH_R
Pause/Play toggle: KC_MPLY

Mute Mic (MS teams) (ctrl + shift + M): LCTL(LSFT(KC_M))
Media Next Track: KC_MNXT
Media Previous Track: KC_MPRV
Pause/Play toggle: KC_MPLY
Copy: KC_Copy
Paste: KC_Paste
